Get to know us

Our Vision and Mission

To be the premier supplier of rice, is deeply ingrained in the hearts of our customers, retailers, and communities.

Achieve profitable growth through superior customer satisfaction, innovation, quality, and unwavering commitment.

At Cow & Calf, we take pride in offering a diverse range of premium rice varieties to meet the discerning tastes of our customers

Under the SKP Group, led by S. Krishna Reddy, we have established market-leading ventures such as Sri Venkateswara Aqua Culture, Sri Venkateswara Aqua Feeds, Sri Ramalingeswara Aqua Feeds, Sri Venkateswara Shrimp Hatcheries Private Limited, Sri Ramalingeswara Commercial Complex, Sri Ramalingeswara Properties Private Limited, and Sri Venkateswara Fertilisers Private Limited.

Our USP lies in our commitment to quality. With our state-of-the-art processing facility equipped with Satake Japan Technology, we ensure that every grain of rice is 100% Sortex clean and packed with utmost hygiene. Our Pure Old Raw Rice, with nil brokens and exceptional quality, stands as a testament to our dedication to excellence.

Welcome to Cow & Calf
Your Trusted Source for Premium Rice

Established in 1982 by S. Krishna Reddy, Sri Ramalingeswara Modern Rice Mill (SRMRM) has been a pioneer in delivering top-quality rice to households worldwide. Under the esteemed brand name of Cow & Calf, we have set industry standards and gained the trust of millions of customers over the past four decades.
