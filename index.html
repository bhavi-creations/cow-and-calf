Welcome to Cow & Calf
Your Trusted Source for Premium Rice

Established in 1982 by S. Krishna Reddy, Sri Ramalingeswara Modern Rice Mill (SRMRM) has been a pioneer in delivering top-quality rice to households worldwide. Under the esteemed brand name of Cow & Calf, we have set industry standards and gained the trust of millions of customers over the past four decades.
